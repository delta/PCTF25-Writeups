Operation Cipherlock

As the world inches closer to the brink of World War III, intelligence agencies are racing against time to secure critical information that could alter the course of the impending conflict. A seasoned operative, Agent Johnny, recently transmitted confidential intelligence. However, it was intercepted by a formidable counterintelligence agency determined to unlock its secrets. Agent Pacheco, your mission is to decipher the intercepted message and uncover the crucial information that could prevent a global catastrophe.
